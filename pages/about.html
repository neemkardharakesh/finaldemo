Getting Started with EXOSUITES

This guide will help you set up and operate the EXOSUITES platform, from selecting hardware components to testing your complete robotic system.

Before You Start - Resources You'll Need

Everything you need to get your EXOSUITES system up and running is available through the recommended hardware and software resources. We assume access to basic mechatronics equipment such as:

  • Keyboard and Mouse
  • Monitor
  • Soldering Iron and Solder
  • Heat Shrink Tubing
  • Wire Cutters and Strippers
  • Soldering Flux
  • Dupont Jumper Wires
  • 24–28 Gauge Spare Wire

First Steps - Get Your Gear

Before starting the tutorials, determine which hardware components best fit your application.

Key Components

  • Computer: Raspberry Pi, NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano
  • Power Source: Drill Battery, LiPo Battery, Power Bank
  • Actuators: CubeMars AK-Series, CyberGear, RobStride
  • Sensors: Microstrain IMUs, OpenIMUs, MPU9250 IMUs

Set Up Your Single Board Computer

To begin this step, you will need your selected computer and an SD card.

Install the operating system, configure required software, and prepare the device for robotic operation.

By the end of this step, you should have a functioning computer with EXOSUITES installed and ready to communicate with your robotic hardware.

Assemble Your Mechatronics

Gather all hardware components selected during the planning phase.

Assemble the power and communication subsystems connecting:

  • Battery Systems
  • Single Board Computer
  • Actuators
  • Sensors
  • Communication Interfaces

Once completed, your system should be fully wired and capable of exchanging data between all devices.

Configure Your Actuators

CubeMars Actuators

  • CubeMars Actuator
  • Windows Computer
  • R-Link Interface
  • CAN + UART Wiring

RobStride / CyberGear

  • Actuator
  • Computer
  • Communication Wiring Setup

Assign a unique CAN ID to each actuator to enable simultaneous multi-actuator control and communication.

After this step, your actuators will be prepared for real-time operation.

Test Your EXOSUITES Robot

Ensure that the complete system is assembled, powered, and running EXOSUITES software.

Use the provided testing scripts to verify communication with actuators and sensors.

  • Verify CAN Communication
  • Check Sensor Readings
  • Validate Actuator Commands
  • Confirm System Stability

Upon successful testing, your robotic platform will be ready for custom controller development and advanced experimentation.

Next Steps

You can now begin developing your own robotic controllers, experimenting with advanced control algorithms, and integrating additional sensors and actuators into the EXOSUITES platform.
